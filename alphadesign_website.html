I'm a creator living in Los Angeles, currently designing, writing, illustrating, or on the lookout for what's next. Thinking up new ideas or projects to tackle is the way I stay happy and make a living. More specifically, I currently work as a Senior Graphic Designer and have taken up front-end web development looking to make more of a pivot towards that. As a fledgling coder, I understand the need to develop design solutions that are actually applicable to web development. Not possible with code? Then it's not good web design. This is not new in terms of knowing that design must be inspiring and pleasing, but mainly fulfill its functional requirements.


In my free time I'm either illustrating, reading Scientific American, drinking LA craft beer, eating tacos, or visiting national and state parks.


Please, do feel free to reach out to say hi, offer employment opportunities, commission an illustration, share a recipe, or ask more about me. No spam, thanks.
